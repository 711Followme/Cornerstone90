# Performance Comparison - Update Schedule

## Before vs After

### Update Checks:
- **Before**: 3,168 checks/day (every 1-5 minutes)
- **After**: 2 checks/day (3 AM & 3 PM CT)
- **Reduction**: 99.94%

### Data Usage:
- **Before**: ~7-36 MB/day
- **After**: ~10 KB/day
- **Reduction**: 99.97%

### Battery Impact:
- **Before**: 5-10% daily drain
- **After**: <0.1% daily drain
- **Improvement**: 95-99% better

### CPU Usage:
- **Before**: Constant background activity
- **After**: 2 brief checks daily
- **Reduction**: 99%+

## Update Schedule

**Twice Daily:**
- 3:00 AM Central Time
- 3:00 PM Central Time

**Benefits:**
- Minimal battery usage
- Lower data consumption
- Better performance
- Still keeps app updated
