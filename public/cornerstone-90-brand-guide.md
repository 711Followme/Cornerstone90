# CORNERSTONE: 90 - Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Name:** CORNERSTONE: 90  
**Tagline:** "90 Days to Build What Lasts"  
**Motto:** "Built on Christ. Rebuilt in Him. Standing Firm."

---

## The Motto

### Primary
> **"Built on Christ. Rebuilt in Him. Standing Firm."**

### Alternatives
1. "The Stone the Builders Rejected Has Become Our Foundation."
2. "Let Us Rise and Build. Christ Is Our Cornerstone."
3. "Broken Men. Solid Foundation. Unshakeable Life."
4. "Founded on the Rock. Built to Last."

### Battle Cry
> **"RISE AND BUILD!"** (Nehemiah 2:18)

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Stone Gray:** #6B7280 (Foundation, strength)
- **Deep Blue:** #1E40AF (Stability, divine)
- **Gold/Amber:** #D97706 (Divine, precious)

### Accent Colors
- **Crimson Red:** #DC2626 (Sacrifice, urgency)
- **Warm White:** #F9FAFB (Purity, renewal)
- **Charcoal:** #374151 (Strength, depth)

---

## Visual Theme

**Design Philosophy:** "Architectural Strength with Biblical Foundation"

**Key Elements:**
- Cornerstone blocks (chiseled appearance)
- Blueprint grid backgrounds
- Construction tools imagery
- Building level progression
- Stone texture patterns

---

## Scripture Foundation

**Christ as Cornerstone:**
- Psalm 118:22 - "The stone the builders rejected"
- Isaiah 28:16 - "A precious cornerstone for a sure foundation"
- Ephesians 2:20 - "Christ Jesus himself as the chief cornerstone"
- 1 Corinthians 3:11 - "No other foundation than...Jesus Christ"

**Nehemiah's Resolve:**
- Nehemiah 2:18 - "Let us start rebuilding"
- Nehemiah 4:14 - "Remember the Lord...and fight"
- Nehemiah 6:3 - "I am carrying on a great project"

**Building to Last:**
- Matthew 7:24-25 - "Built his house on the rock"
- Psalm 127:1 - "Unless the LORD builds the house"

---

## Mission Statement

> "Cornerstone: 90 is a brotherhood of men rebuilding their lives on the only foundation that lasts—Jesus Christ. Through 90 days of discipline, Scripture, and brotherhood, we become the men God designed us to be: strong, faithful, and unshakeable."

---

## Core Values

1. **Christ as Cornerstone** - Everything built on Him
2. **Nehemiah's Resolve** - Rise and build despite opposition
3. **Brotherhood** - Iron sharpens iron
4. **Discipline** - Daily obedience, not motivation
5. **Legacy** - Building what lasts beyond 90 days

---

## Program Structure

### Phase 1: Laying the Foundation (Days 1-30)
- Focus: Christ as Cornerstone
- Nehemiah: Chapters 1-3

### Phase 2: Raising the Walls (Days 31-60)
- Focus: Character and Discipline
- Nehemiah: Chapters 4-8

### Phase 3: Securing the Structure (Days 61-90)
- Focus: Leadership and Legacy
- Nehemiah: Chapters 9-13

---

## Marketing Copy

### Elevator Pitch
"Cornerstone: 90 is a 90-day journey for men who are ready to rebuild. Built on Christ, guided by Nehemiah's example, supported by brothers. Daily Scripture, intentional workouts, relentless discipline. You'll emerge unshakeable."

### Hero Copy
Your life may be in ruins. But the foundation—Jesus Christ—is unshakeable. In 90 days, you'll rebuild on the only cornerstone that lasts. No shortcuts. No excuses. Just solid construction, day by day.

---

## Brand Voice

**Masculine but Not Toxic:** Strong, direct, accountable  
**Honest but Hopeful:** Acknowledge brokenness, point to restoration  
**Biblical but Practical:** Scripture-saturated, immediately applicable  
**Brotherhood but Individual:** Together yet unique
