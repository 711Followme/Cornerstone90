## Men's Alliance Core Values (Cycled Throughout)

1. **Honor**: Do not lie, cheat, steal, or laugh at sin.
2. **Encouragement**: No matter what happens you can always say, "Good"
3. **Trust**: Give others the benefit of the doubt.
4. **Feedback**: Seek feedback from others, Become comfortable receiving feedback from others.
5. **Humility**: It's better to under sell and over deliver.
6. **Perseverance**: Whether in a workout, or in our lives, we strive to leave a legacy of never quitting, of getting back up every time

## Exodus Journey Arc

**The Pattern of Deliverance:**
1. **Bondage** - Comfortable slavery
2. **Call** - God's invitation to freedom
3. **Confrontation** - Facing what enslaves you
4. **Deliverance** - God's miraculous intervention
5. **Wilderness** - Testing and refinement
6. **Covenant** - Binding commitment to God
7. **Consecration** - Set apart for God's purposes

**Key Exodus Themes:**
- Freedom from bondage
- God's power over false gods
- Daily dependence on God
- Learning to fight
- God's law and holiness
- The cost of freedom
- God's presence among His people
- Failure and restoration

## Application Patterns

Each day includes:
- **Scripture Passage**: Specific verses from Exodus
- **Reflection**: Deep dive into meaning for modern men breaking free from comfort
- **Application**: Specific, actionable steps toward freedom
- **Prayer**: Honest, masculine prayer for deliverance
- **Men's Alliance Value**: One of six core values

## Exodus vs. Nehemiah

**Nehemiah Focus**: Rebuilding what's broken
**Exodus Focus**: Breaking free from bondage

**Nehemiah Journey**: From ruins to restoration
**Exodus Journey**: From slavery to freedom

**Nehemiah Man**: The rebuilder
**Exodus Man**: The freed slave becoming a warrior

**Best For**:
- **Nehemiah**: Men rebuilding after failure, restoring broken areas
- **Exodus**: Men breaking free from addiction, comfort, or bondage
